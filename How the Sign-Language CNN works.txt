How the Sign-Language CNN works:

A CNN is essentially a series of filters that looks for patterns
The CNN doesn't look at the entire image at once, it chooses a small window and traces that window across the whole image looking for certain lines or curves
Later layers then combine those lines or curves into shapes and attempts to recognize that whole shape as something like "a thumb"

After the filters identified all the shapes it sends the "flattened" information to model.py, specifically, self.classifier
This classifies the image as certain letters, for example it will see folded fingers and the thumb on the outside and say thats "A"
